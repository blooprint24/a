Common Questions

Are you insured?
Yes! Auto Shine and Pressure Washing is fully insured. Ask us about our coverage details during your booking.
Do I need to be home?
For exterior pressure washing, you don't need to be home as long as we have access to the area. For auto detailing, we'll need keys and access to the vehicle.
How long does it take?
A full detail typically takes 3-5 hours depending on the vehicle condition. Pressure washing depends on the size of the area.
